## Repair Artifact

Created `scripts/quantum_shear_primitive_search.py`.

The script normal-orders the matrix Weyl algebra exactly over
`fractions.Fraction`, with
\[
[X^i{}_j,Y^k{}_l]=\hbar\delta^i_l\delta^k_j,\qquad
Y^k{}_lX^i{}_j=X^i{}_jY^k{}_l-\hbar\delta^i_l\delta^k_j .
\]
It constructs the shuffle Weyl trace \(J_N(z_1^az_2^b)\), the Moyal
bracket trace using the odd-order coefficient formula from the appendix,
and the defect \(E_{a,b,N}\).  It then searches first in the covariant
left-ideal ansatz
\[
\sum_W c_W\operatorname{Tr}(W M)
=\sum_{i,j}\left(\sum_W c_W W^j{}_i\right)M^i{}_j ,
\]
and falls back to a brute-force normal-monomial left-ideal solve at fixed
Weyl weight.  Both searches use sparse rational elimination and verify the
reconstructed polynomial against \(E_{a,b,N}\).

## Theorem-Grade Next Step

The finite data points to the following precise universal repair:

Find coefficients \(c_W^{a,b}\in\mathbb Q\), indexed by words \(W\) with
\(a-1\) letters \(X\) and \(b-1\) letters \(Y\), such that in the
universal matrix Weyl algebra
\[
E_{a,b,N}=\sum_W c_W^{a,b}\operatorname{Tr}(W M)
\quad\text{for every }N.
\]
Then \(A_{ij}^{a,b,N}=\sum_W c_W^{a,b}W^j{}_i\) is the required left
primitive.

The proof should not be a finite-rank extrapolation.  It should be a
PBW-normal-ordering identity in the free indexed Weyl algebra, or a
rank-stable trace identity proved before specializing to \(N\).  The
computational conjecture worth attacking next is stronger than mere ideal
membership: the primitive appears to be hbar-free in the covariant word
basis; all Moyal hbar-corrections arise from contractions with \(M\).
