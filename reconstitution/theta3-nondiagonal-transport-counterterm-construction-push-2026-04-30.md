## Construction attempted

The only canonical lower-window source ancestor in the current data is the
same two-\(u\) source used by Agents 215, 245, and 257:
\[
  \zeta^0_2=u_{a,H_{1,0}}u_{b,H_{0,1}},
  \qquad H_{p,q}=z_1^pz_2^q .
\]
With
\[
  [H_{a,b},H_{r,s}]=(as-br)H_{a+r-1,b+s-1},
\]
direct finite-window CE calculation gives
\[
  d_{\mathrm{CE}}\zeta^0_2
    =2\zeta_{\nu_{02}}-2\zeta_{\nu_{20}} .
\]
Since the row convention is
\[
  E^2_{\nu}=-K^2_{\Theta_3}(\zeta_{2,\nu}),
\]
the only non-arbitrary lower-window primitive formula derivable from the
source coordinates is
\[
  D^2_{02,20}:=K^2_{\Theta_3}(\zeta^0_2).
\]
If the Hom-valued Bianchi column
\[
  \mathfrak b^2_{02,20}
  :=
  d_{\mathrm{ns},2}K^2_{\Theta_3}(\zeta^0_2)
  -K^2_{\Theta_3}(d_{\mathrm{CE}}\zeta^0_2)
\]
vanishes, then
\[
\begin{aligned}
  d_{\mathrm{ns},2}D^2_{02,20}
    &=K^2_{\Theta_3}(2\zeta_{\nu_{02}}-2\zeta_{\nu_{20}})\\
    &=-2E^2_{\nu_{02}}+2E^2_{\nu_{20}} .
\end{aligned}
\]
Thus the lower-window primitive has a canonical formula, but it is not a
current local-functional repair until the package supplies the local
functional \(K^2_{\Theta_3}(\zeta^0_2)\), its scalar-zero marker value, its
transport, and the identity \(\mathfrak b^2_{02,20}=0\).

## Non-diagonal transport test

Keep the source-coordinate first two columns fixed and let the six
degree-three columns be arbitrary:
\[
  v_3,\ldots,v_8\in\C^2 .
\]
Killing \(r_8\) requires
\[
  3v_3+2v_4-v_5+v_6-2v_7-3v_8=(-2,2)^T .
\]
This affine space is nonempty; for example a theta-column correction
\[
  v_6=(-2,2)^T,\qquad v_i=0\ (i\ne6)
\]
would kill the vector. It is not a construction from the current data.
The current source-face coordinates force every degree-three column to
zero under \(N=2\) projection, and the script records no projected
renormalized weights or lower row-basis reduction from which a non-diagonal
block could be derived. A non-diagonal \(v^{M,2}\) is therefore an extra
marked Costello transport datum, not a consequence of the present
coordinates.

## Exact obstruction theorem

Let \(\mathcal H^{\mathrm{coord}}_{\theta_3}\) be the habitat generated by:

- the eight current source-face rows and their free monomial coordinates;
- the source-coordinate finite-window projection;
- the current primitive slots checked by `scripts/finite_window_graph_array.py`;
- no additional marked Costello incidence/weight table;
- no local functional \(K^2_{\Theta_3}(\zeta^0_2)\) with verified
  Hom-valued Bianchi column;
- no lower-window row relation \(E^2_{\nu_{02}}=E^2_{\nu_{20}}\).

Then the \(N=2\) transported eight-face residual is not killed in
\(\mathcal H^{\mathrm{coord}}_{\theta_3}\). The obstruction is the pair
\[
  \left(
    \lambda^{(2)}_{\nu_{02}},
    \mathfrak b^2_{02,20}
  \right),
\]
where
\[
  \lambda^{(2)}_{\nu_{02}}(2E^2_{\nu_{02}}-2E^2_{\nu_{20}})=1
\]
certifies non-exactness in the current lower primitive slots, and
\(\mathfrak b^2_{02,20}\) is the exact Hom-curvature/Bianchi row whose
vanishing would promote
\[
  D^2_{02,20}=K^2_{\Theta_3}(u_{a,H_{1,0}}u_{b,H_{0,1}})
\]
to a genuine scalar-zero local primitive. Current source-face coordinates
give only the diagonal cutoff. Any non-diagonal matrix killing \(r_8\)
requires additional projected renormalized weight coordinates or a chosen
lower row presentation; neither is present in the current package.

## Whether a real repair was constructed

A real lower source primitive formula was isolated:
\[
  D^2_{02,20}=K^2_{\Theta_3}(u_{a,H_{1,0}}u_{b,H_{0,1}}).
\]
The finite matrix check shows that this column would kill the \(N=2\)
transport obstruction exactly. It is not yet a current local-functional
counterterm, because the required Hom-valued Bianchi identity
\(\mathfrak b^2_{02,20}=0\), scalar-zero marker value, codimension-two
closure, and primitive transport are not supplied by the checkout.

Thus no full theta3 repair was constructed. The push past Agent 257 is the
sharper obstruction/gate: the lower residual is source-exact, and the sole
remaining lower primitive obstruction is the named Hom-curvature row
\(\mathfrak b^2_{02,20}\) plus the absent scalar-zero/transport data.

```tex
  \(\zeta^0_2=u_{a,H_{1,0}}u_{b,H_{0,1}}\).  In the degree-\(2\)

      -K^2_{\Theta_3}(d_{\mathrm{CE}}\zeta^0_2)

  marked habitat with compatible transport.  Without that datum the current
  lower row presentation has no degree-zero column, and
  \(\frac12(E^2_{\nu_{02}})^*\) detects the residual
  \(2E^2_{\nu_{02}}-2E^2_{\nu_{20}}\).
\end{prop}
```
