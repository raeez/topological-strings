# LQT Scalar-Koszul Quotient Audit

Date: 2026-04-30

Scope: finite-window Loday--Quillen--Tsygan habitat for
`A_psi=T(x,y,psi)`, `d psi=xy-yx`, as recorded in
`theorem-lanes.tex`, `claim-strength-ledger.tex`, and
`open-obligations.tex`.  Report-only audit.  No TeX edited.

## Verdict

The corrected habitat is sound as a local finite-window construction:

```text
A_{psi,W}=A_psi/F^{>W}A_psi
```

with `F^{>W}` the two-sided dg ideal generated by words of total
Koszul weight `>W`, for

```text
wt(x)=(1,0), wt(y)=(0,1), wt(psi)=(1,1).
```

This fixes the window defect.  Ordinary letter-length cutoffs are not
dg-stable, since `psi` has ordinary length one and `d psi=xy-yx` has
ordinary length two.  The Koszul bidegree and the total Koszul weight
are preserved by the differential.

The finite-window comparison is theorem-grade only on the stable
single-cycle image:

```text
Theta_{N,W}: CC_red(A_{psi,W})[1]
  -> C_*^CE(gl_N(A_{psi,W}))^{GL_N},
Prim^{1cyc}_{N,W}:=im Theta_{N,W},
lambda_{LQT,W}=Theta_{N,W}^{-1}
```

after the stable range controls both the Koszul window and CE length.
On all stable invariant CE chains the expression
`Theta_{N,W}^{-1} P_{1cyc}` is meaningful only after constructing the
connected single-cycle projection `P_{1cyc}` as a chain projection
compatible with the finite-window tower.

The scalar quotient is the remaining load-bearing point.  Killing the
scalar one-`psi` class alone is false at chain level:

```text
d_CE Theta_1(psi)=Theta_1(xy-yx),
lambda d_CE Theta_1(psi)=[xy-yx]=d_CC[psi].
```

Thus the Hamiltonian scalar quotient must either be postponed to
homology or must kill the two-term scalar-Koszul subcomplex on both
source and target:

```text
Theta_1(psi) -> Theta_1(xy-yx),
[psi] -> [xy-yx].
```

The current TeX records this correctly at `theorem-lanes.tex:2102-2105`,
`claim-strength-ledger.tex:408-410`, and `open-obligations.tex:160-162`.

## Attacks

### A1. LQ84/Tsygan do not prove the dg finite-window claim

Status: valid attack; fenced.

Loday--Quillen 1984 and Tsygan 1983 support the stable matrix Lie
homology/cyclic homology template

```text
Prim H_n^CE(gl_infty(A)) = HC_{n-1}(A)
```

for unital associative algebras in characteristic zero.  They do not
prove the manuscript's finite-window dg map for
`A_psi=T(x,y,psi)`, the scalar-Koszul quotient, the connected
single-trace projection, or the open `A_infinity` transfer.  The source
fixture states this boundary at
`references/primary-sources/lqt-tsygan-source-anchors-2026-04-30.md:91-105`
and `:197-212`.

Heal: treat the finite-window map in `theorem-lanes.tex:2079-2105` as an
internal construction in the corrected habitat, not as a direct imported
LQT theorem.

### A2. Unqualified finite-N `Prim C_*^CE` is not a chain-level domain

Status: valid attack; mostly healed.

Literal primitives of the CE coalgebra are CE-length one.  The LQT
primitive is a stable connected Hopf-homology or single-cycle notion.
Therefore a map

```text
Prim C_*^CE(gl_N(A_{psi,W})) -> CC_red(A_{psi,W})[1]
```

is undefined until `Prim` is replaced by `Prim^{1cyc}_{N,W}=im Theta`
or until a connected single-cycle projection is constructed.

Heal: the current theorem-lane text says "stable single-cycle image" and
the ledger explicitly sets
`Prim^{1cyc}_{N,W}=im Theta_{N,W}`.  The remaining proof obligation is
to inscribe the chain projection `P_{1cyc}` and its compatibility with
`d_CE`.

### A3. The ordinary finite window is not a dg algebra

Status: valid attack; healed by the Koszul-weight quotient.

If the window is an ordinary word-length subspace, `d psi=xy-yx` leaves
the window.  The quotient by the two-sided ideal generated by total
Koszul weight `>W` is a finite-dimensional unital dg algebra because the
differential preserves weight and multiplication only increases weight.

Heal: `theorem-lanes.tex:2081-2089`,
`claim-strength-ledger.tex:391-396`, and `open-obligations.tex:143-150`
now use the dg quotient habitat.

### A4. Killing `Theta_1(psi)` alone is not a scalar quotient

Status: valid attack; healed as an obstruction.

The obstruction is the explicit chain:

```text
O_scalar(W,N)=d_CE Theta_1(psi)=Theta_1(xy-yx).
```

It is zero only after killing the differential target as part of the
same scalar-Koszul subcomplex, or after passing to homology.  In the
necklace `H_0` shadow `[xy-yx]` vanishes, but in the full reduced cyclic
chain complex it is the internal differential of `[psi]`.

Heal: the current manuscript-facing files state the two-term
scalar-Koszul quotient.  No false scalar quotient remains in the
checked target lines.

### A5. `lambda_{LQT,W}` on all stable invariant CE chains needs `P_{1cyc}`

Status: open proof obligation.

On the image of `Theta_{N,W}`, `lambda=Theta^{-1}` is a chain map once
`Theta` is a chain map and injective in the stated stable range.  On all
stable invariant CE chains, `lambda=Theta^{-1}P_{1cyc}` additionally
requires:

```text
P_{1cyc}^2=P_{1cyc},
d_CE P_{1cyc}=P_{1cyc} d_CE,
im P_{1cyc}=im Theta_{N,W}.
```

This is not supplied by LQ84/Tsygan for the dg finite-window algebra.
It must be proved by finite-window invariant theory plus the dg
trace-cycle calculation.

## Remaining theorem obligations

1. Define the reduced cyclic dg model `CC_red(A_{psi,W})`, including
   Hochschild/internal differential signs, Connes convention if used,
   and the `[1]` shift.
2. Prove the trace-cycle map `Theta_{N,W}` is a dg chain map with the
   chosen CE and cyclic signs.
3. Prove finite-window injectivity of `Theta_{N,W}` in an explicit
   stable range.  The shorthand `N` dominates weight and CE length is
   acceptable as a ledger phrase; the theorem needs a displayed bound
   and proof.
4. Construct `P_{1cyc}` on stable invariant CE chains and prove it is a
   chain projection compatible with the finite-window tower.
5. Prove the scalar-Koszul quotient descends on source and target, and
   that it matches the Hamiltonian scalar quotient in the ordered
   operations
   `Prim_st`, `Conn_{N->infty}`, `pi_Ham`.
6. If the manuscript wants a homological LQT theorem for dg
   `A_{psi,W}`, derive it locally by a filtered/spectral-sequence
   argument or add a primary source anchor.  The present source fixture
   does not supply it.
7. The open `A_infinity` theorem remains separate: construct `A_op`,
   the twisting cochain `tau_op`, the completed cobar morphism, and the
   exact cone/Roos obstruction complex.  The finite-window LQT repair
   closes only the LQT habitat component of that acceptance datum.
