## Decision

The missing scalar-zero counterterm \(B^2_{02,20}\) is not constructed in
the current finite-window local-functional habitat. The exact fixed-window
obstruction is the same Bianchi-exposed cokernel, now applied directly to
the target \(-\mathfrak b^2_{02,20}\).

Work in the generous lower habitat which already adjoins the
source-coordinate candidate
\[
  D^2_{02,20}=K^2_{\Theta_3}(u_{a,H_{1,0}}u_{b,H_{0,1}})
\]
and exposes the Bianchi row. In the ordered degree-one basis
\[
  (E^2_{\nu_{02}},E^2_{\nu_{20}},\mathfrak b^2_{02,20})
\]
the only current degree-zero lower column has boundary
\[
  d_{\mathrm{ns},2}D^2_{02,20}
  =
  -2E^2_{\nu_{02}}+2E^2_{\nu_{20}}
  +\mathfrak b^2_{02,20},
  \qquad
  A_D^2=(-2,2,1)^T .
\]
The sought counterterm would be a supplied scalar-zero local functional
\[
  B^2_{02,20}\in \mathcal K^0_{\mathrm{ns},2}(I),
  \qquad
  d_{\mathrm{ns},2}B^2_{02,20}=-\mathfrak b^2_{02,20}.
\]
In coordinates this asks whether
\[
  (0,0,-1)^T\in \operatorname{im}(A_D^2).
\]
It is not. The first two coordinates force the coefficient of \(D^2_{02,20}\)
to be \(0\); the Bianchi coordinate then forces it to be \(-1\).

The cokernel certificate is
\[
  \widetilde\lambda_{02,\mathfrak b}
  =
  \frac12(E^2_{\nu_{02}})^*
  +(\mathfrak b^2_{02,20})^* .
\]
It satisfies
\[
  \widetilde\lambda_{02,\mathfrak b}A_D^2=0,\qquad
  \widetilde\lambda_{02,\mathfrak b}(-\mathfrak b^2_{02,20})=-1.
\]
Therefore \(\mathfrak b^2_{02,20}\) is a nonzero cohomology class in the
displayed finite row complex. A symbol \(B^2_{02,20}\) without a new boundary
column \(A_B^2=(0,0,-1)^T\) is not a counterterm.

## Habitat Boundary

This obstruction is finite-window and habitat-relative. It proves
nonexistence inside the current data-derived local-functional span:

1. the original theta3 one-row package has no degree-zero term;
2. the guarded CE-ancestor and local-counterterm payloads accepted by the
   validator are interface fixtures, not current data;
3. the Bianchi-exposed lower package adds \(D^2_{02,20}\) and the row
   \(\mathfrak b^2_{02,20}\), but no additional local counterterm column;
4. no marked Costello incidence/weight table, no lower row relation
   \(E^2_{\nu_{02}}=E^2_{\nu_{20}}\), and no non-diagonal transport data are
   supplied.

If a future package supplies a genuine local functional
\[
  B^2_{02,20}
\]
with scalar projection zero, locality or wavefront admissibility, and
boundary column
\[
  A_B^2=(0,0,-1)^T,
\]
then \(D^2_{02,20}+B^2_{02,20}\) kills the lower residual
\[
  r_2=2E^2_{\nu_{02}}-2E^2_{\nu_{20}}.
\]
That is the positive construction target. It is not present now.

## Bianchi Identity Does Not Supply The Primitive

The Hom-valued Bianchi identity gives closure information for the curvature
row. It does not give a contracting homotopy. In the finite-row language, it
can at most justify that \(\mathfrak b^2_{02,20}\) is a degree-one cocycle
after the lower rows are closed. Exactness is the separate equation
\[
  d_{\mathrm{ns},2}B^2_{02,20}=-\mathfrak b^2_{02,20}.
\]
The finite-row primitive-search interface reduces that equation to a
boundary-matrix image statement. The image statement fails by
\(\widetilde\lambda_{02,\mathfrak b}\).

If the Bianchi row is not closed in a proposed larger package, then the
counterterm problem is not yet formed. If it is closed but no column
\((0,0,-1)^T\) is supplied, the obstruction above applies.

## Tower Gate

A fixed-window \(B^2_{02,20}\) would still not be the full theorem. For a
tower of candidates \(B^M\), compatibility requires
\[
  d_{\mathrm{ns},N}(\pi_{M,N}B^M-B^N)
  =
  -\pi_{M,N}\mathfrak b^M+\mathfrak b^N.
\]
Thus a compatible family exists only after the Bianchi defects transport
strictly, or after the right-hand side is itself supplied as a boundary. The
current data do not contain this Bianchi-transport homotopy.

## Exact Obstruction Statement

Let \(\mathcal H^{B,2}_{\theta_3,\mathrm{cur}}\) be the degree-two
Bianchi-exposed finite row habitat generated by:

- rows \(E^2_{\nu_{02}},E^2_{\nu_{20}},\mathfrak b^2_{02,20}\);
- the single lower source-coordinate column \(D^2_{02,20}\) with
  \(A_D^2=(-2,2,1)^T\);
- no additional degree-zero local-functional column;
- no row relation among the three displayed rows;
- no non-diagonal transport or codimension-two marked table.

Then no \(B^2_{02,20}\in\mathcal H^{B,2}_{\theta_3,\mathrm{cur}}\) satisfies
\[
  d_{\mathrm{ns},2}B^2_{02,20}=-\mathfrak b^2_{02,20}.
\]
The obstruction class is detected by
\[
  \widetilde\lambda_{02,\mathfrak b}
  =
  \frac12(E^2_{\nu_{02}})^*
  +(\mathfrak b^2_{02,20})^* .
\]
This is not a universal no-go for the full Costello local-functional
complex. It is the exact obstruction for the current finite-window package.
