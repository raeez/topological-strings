Verified anchors:

- p. 39 abstract: Koszul resolutions generalize the classical Koszul
  resolutions; `K*(A)` is an explicit quotient of the cobar resolution
  whose homology gives the cohomology algebra.
- pp. 42--43, Section 2: definitions of pre-Koszul, homogeneous Koszul,
  and Koszul algebra via the diagonal condition on `H^{s,p-*}A`.

- p. 48, Theorem 3.8: for Koszul `A`, `Tor(R,L)` is the homology of the
  Koszul complex.
- p. 48, Proposition 3.9: canonical injection of Koszul complex into the
  bar resolution.
- p. 48, Proposition 3.10: injection of differential coalgebras into the
  bar construction.
- p. 51, Theorem 5.3: a PBW algebra is homogeneous Koszul.

Hypotheses.  Ordinary algebraic Koszul/PBW setting with a chosen
generator/relations presentation and the homogeneity/Koszul hypotheses
in the paper.

Supported manuscript use.  Classical Koszul resolution vocabulary,
PBW-implies-Koszul source support, and finite algebraic universal
enveloping examples.

Non-support boundary.  Priddy does not construct a Tate completion,
lower-central Tate-pronilpotent dg Lie algebra, exact continuous
dualization, completed Hamiltonian envelope, admissible model envelope,
finite-window ML/Roos gate, or strict endpoint obstruction.
