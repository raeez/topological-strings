Scope.  This file routes compact Calabi-Yau source obligations that are
not Hall, CoHA, or centre vocabulary.  It is not a primary-source mirror
and it closes no theorem.  Its role is to prevent non-CoHA compact-CY
facts from being carried by
`references/primary-sources/coha-center-source-anchors-2026-04-30.md`.

Status.  The existing CoHA fixture is restricted to Lurie centre
grammar, Joyce and Gross--Joyce--Tanaka Hall/vertex vocabulary,
Joyce--Upmeier orientation prerequisites, Kinjo--Park--Safronov 3CY
CoHA vocabulary, and Costello--Gwilliam factorization vocabulary used
only to delimit centre language.  Everything below belongs to separate
compact-CY source lanes.

Supremum Discipline.  A routed source row remains an obstruction
component until its fixture gives local source anchors, conventions, and
non-support statements.  Routing does not kill the source obstruction,
does not construct `C_tar`, and does not supply an
`Ob_UKD(C_tar)` null-homotopy.

compact target data named without domain-specific source claims:

- CDGP mirror-quintic periods and Abel-Jacobi comparison data.
- GV BPS/resummation formulae.
- OSV attractor-rate statements.

- Local arithmetic ledgers and historical low-degree quintic source rows.

target objects, trace maps, centre automorphisms, rigidity, orientation
compatibility for the frontier target, or `Ob_UKD(C_tar)=0`.
