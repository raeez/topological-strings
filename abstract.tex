\begin{abstract}
We study the formal local mixed holomorphic-topological brane at the
symplectic disk with coordinates \(z_1,z_2\) and form
\(dz_1\wedge dz_2\). On a stack of \(N\) probes these coordinates are
matrices \(\phi_1,\phi_2\in\mathfrak{gl}_N\), and the open ghost-zero
system is the Dirac first-order theory
\[
  \int_\R\operatorname{Tr}\bigl(\phi_1\,d\phi_2
  +A[\phi_1,\phi_2]\bigr)
\]
with first-class constraint \([\phi_1,\phi_2]=0\) and BV/Koszul
variable \(\psi=A^*\) satisfying \(Q\psi=[\phi_1,\phi_2]\). Closed
Hamiltonian modes act through the reduced CE/PV Hamiltonian
central-operation model. A full \(P_0\)-factorization-centre theorem for the
unreduced open theory is not asserted. The cotangent modes are not polynomial
descendants: in the local theorem they are Grothendieck--Matlis
residue currents in
\[
  \mathcal P=\operatorname{Ann}_{\mathrm{res}}(\C\cdot1)\subset
  H^2_{\mathfrak m}(\C[[z_1,z_2]])\,dz_1dz_2
  =\bigoplus_{a+b>0}\C\,\rho_{a,b}.
\]
The stable polynomial one-\(\psi\) classes \(\Psi_{a,b}\) are PBW
special-fibre open labels; they are not another basis of
\(\mathcal P\) as an \(\mathfrak h\)-module.

The finite algebraic part proves the Dirac brane reduction, the
scalar-reduced degree-zero Weyl/Moyal comparison, and the scalar
\(U(1)\) anomaly. The degreewise stable part proves the PBW trace
sector, the cochain-level CE/PV derived-centre map for
\(\mathfrak h=\C[[z_1,z_2]]/\C\cdot1\), the Matlis principal-part
cotangent module, the compactly supported brane-line current model, and
the principal-part defect model after de Rham-current contraction. The weighted Tate
construction is proved only in the weighted Tate coefficient model and
makes no regulator-independence claim. The first and third Costello
graph normalizations are reduced open-line Weyl/Moyal boundary
coefficient computations to stated order. The all-order brane-defect
graph realization is conditional on a mixed brane-defect QME theorem,
the radial-parts input where finite \(N\) is used, and the required
centrality homotopies. The unreduced
cotangent factorization-centre morphism, a one-antifield descendant
Moyal lift, a Rees/Fourier bridge between \(\Psi_{a,b}\) and
\(\rho_{a,b}\), compact Calabi--Yau consequences, BKM/Igusa
consequences, and sister-volume transfers are not asserted here.
\end{abstract}
